Mayawati Ashram Lohaghat

Very quaint and peaceful place. This Vivekananda Ashram is around 125 years old. Very well maintained, neat and clean. It has a beautiful garden, meditation room, a library and a mueseum along with residentail quarters for the devotees who live there.


They also have small vegetable garden of there own which at the time of our visit had maize, sweet corns, cucumbers etc vegetables.


The location of the ashram is such that the views of the himalayan valley is mesmerizing. This is a must visit place.


Photography is strictly prohibited at this place.
