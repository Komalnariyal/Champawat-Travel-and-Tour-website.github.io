KRANTESHWAR
TEMPLE

  • KRANTESHWAR, UTTARAKHAND
  • Locality/village : Champawat
  • State : Uttarakhand
  • Country : India
  • Nearest City/Town : lohaghat
  • Best Season To Visit : All
  • Languages : Hindi & English
  • Timings : 9 AM to 11.30 AM and 5 PM to 8.30 PM.
  • Photography : Allowed

Kranteshwar Mahadev Temple is one of the holiest shrines that are constructed in the state of Uttarakhand. The temple is located atop a lofty hill on the east of Champawat town and is locally addressed by the name of the temple of Lord Kandev and Lord Kurmapad. The temple is uniquely carved and is positioned at an elevated height of 6,100 mts above sea level and is located at a distance of 6kms from the main market of Champawat

Kranteshwar Mahadev Temple -

Lord Kumapad and Lord Temple This temple was built in the ancient times and is one of the significant tourist spots for many. The main residing deity of this temple is Lord Shiva and doles out pleasing views to snow clad Himalayas and ensures a mesmerizing travelling experience. Devotees who arrive here to worship Lord Shiva offer milk, desi ghee and curd to God Kandev.


It is a beautiful temple whose architectures reminds of the ancient era. It is completely besieged by lush emerald settings and is a perfect place for devotees to sit back and relax in the lap of Mother Nature.


How to Arrive at Kranteshwar Mahadev Temple Champawat


To arrive at Kranteshwar Mahadev temple one can travel via taxi or can take a trek from the town. The shrine is only 6kms away on the East of Champawat Town and is well connected by robust network of motorable roads from Tanakpur.

• Closest railway station is Tanakpur station- 82kms • Closest airport is Pantnagar airport- 174kms
